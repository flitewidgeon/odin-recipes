Meatballs

Description

Thes meatballs are juicy, tender, and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1kg mince
  • 1 cup grated cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 tbsp chopped Italian flat leaf parsley
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups bread crumbs
  • 1 1/2 cup lukewarm water
  • 1 cup olive oil

Steps

  1. Mix together mince, cheese, eggs, garlic, parsley, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Add the breadcrumbs and slowly mix in water.
  3. Shape the mixture into meatballs.
  4. Fry the meatballs in the oil until they are brown and crisp.
