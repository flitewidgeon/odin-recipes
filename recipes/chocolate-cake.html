Chocolate Cake

Description

A chocolate cake made with cocoa and hot coffee.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 1 cup hot strong coffee

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 175 degrees C. Grease and flour a 23 x 33cm baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, stir together flour, sugar, baking powder, and baking soda.
  3. Add oil, eggs, and vanilla. Mix until smooth.
  4. Stir in hot coffee last.
  5. Spread mixture evenly in baking dish.
  6. Bake for 25 to 35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into cake comes out clean.
